02 · Method

Movement is
a form of listening.

After a decade guiding people through rehabilitation in Slovenia, from university athletes to older adults in long‑term care, I moved my practice to Los Realejos to work with different light, different air, different pace.

I combine therapeutic exercise, soft‑tissue techniques and manual manipulation. The premise is simple: the body holds its own intelligence and often only needs to be given the right context to recover.

Every session starts by listening. Every plan is built around the real anatomy of the person in front of me. The tools are well known; the difficult part, and the valuable one, is the precision with which they are applied.

03 · Areas

What I can
work with.

The range is wide: post‑surgical recovery, chronic pain, postural imbalance, and active people who want to improve performance or avoid injury. If you are not sure whether your case fits, write to me.

01

Injury rehabilitation

Post‑operative recovery, sprains, tendinopathies, sports injuries. Phased protocols combining progressive exercise and manual release.

02

Chronic conditions

Low‑back pain, neck pain, disc herniation, fibromyalgia, osteoarthritis. Work centred on functional capacity and gradual pain reduction.

03

Posture and movement

Detailed kinesiological assessment, correction of compensatory patterns, one‑to‑one corrective exercise.

04

Performance and prevention

Plans for active people and athletes: conditioning, injury prevention and recovery massage.

05

Older adults

Mobility, balance and functional strength. Keeping autonomy and quality of life at the clinic or at home.

06

General wellbeing

Exercise as medicine. Energy, rest, balance and relaxation, supported by guidance on everyday physical habits.
