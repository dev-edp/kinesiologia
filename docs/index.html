02 · Method

Movement is
a form of listening.

After more than a decade working with people in rehabilitation in Slovenia — university athletes, people in chronic care, post-surgical recovery — I moved my practice to Los Realejos for a different light, air and rhythm.

I combine therapeutic exercise, massage techniques and manual therapy. The body has its own intelligence; sometimes it just needs the right context to recover.

03 · Areas

What I can
support.

A wide range: post-surgical recovery, chronic pain, postural imbalance — and active people who want to move better, prevent injury and feel more energy. Not sure if your case fits? Just write.

01

Injury rehabilitation

Post-op, sprains, tendinopathies, sports injuries. Phased protocols, progressive exercise, manual release.

02

Chronic conditions

Low-back pain, neck pain, disc herniation, fibromyalgia, osteoarthritis. Functional capacity, gradual pain reduction.

03

Posture & movement

Detailed kinesiological assessment, correction of compensation patterns, individual corrective exercise.

04

Performance & prevention

Plans for active people and athletes: conditioning, injury prevention, recovery massage.

05

Older adults

Mobility, balance, functional strength. Independence and quality of life — at the practice or at home.

06

General wellbeing

Exercise as medicine. Energy, rest, balance, relaxation — with simple guidance on daily habits.

05 · For businesses

Workplace ergonomics.

On-site assessment for offices, hotels, ateliers and small teams. Practical adjustments and short routines that reduce pain, fatigue and time off.

Workplace audit

Walk-through & assessment

A site visit covering desks, screens, chairs, lifting and movement patterns. Written report with prioritized changes.

Team workshop

A 60–90 min session with the team: simple exercises, breathing, daily routines that fit the workflow.
